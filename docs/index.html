Who I Am

Hi, I’m Henna Venho, and I’m passionate about coding and finding my place in the IT industry. I aspire towards cybersecurity and sustainable development in software engineering. I have studied full-stack programming since March 2024 at kood/Sisu, where we implement real-life projects in quite a fast pace. I am proud of how far me and my team-mate have come in such a short time, and want focus on the potential we have for growing and learning quickly in the future as well. Please check out my projects.

I have a Master’s degree in English, and being now in my early 40s and having started my work career working a summer job every year since the age of 12, I have accumulated almost 30 years of work experience. I’ve done pretty much everything related to supply chain management, from production planning to shipping, and I’ve worked in customer service for the sales organizations of nearly 150 countries.

I’ve received remarkable feedback especially on my communication skills, and soft skills overall. I am also well-organized and detail-oriented, as well as tenacious at problem-solving.

In the workplace, I especially value good team spirit and teamwork, effective communication, authenticity, fairness, and mutual flexibility.
